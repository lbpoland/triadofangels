About Triad of Angels

Hope. Strength. Fire. Innovation.

Our Story

Where hope, strength, and fire meet creativity.

Who We Are

Triad of Angels was born from a vision: to create a movement that transcends music and media, weaving together sound, visuals, technology, and pure creative fire. We are not just a band—we are a force for inspiration, strength, and innovation in a world that craves authenticity.

Our Mission

We aim to push the boundaries of what's possible across music, gaming, visual media, apps, and more. Our mission is to inspire, empower, and uplift through every beat, every visual, every story we tell.

About ToA Studios

ToA Studios is the creative umbrella and record label behind Triad of Angels. While Triad of Angels is our flagship band—bringing angelic harmonies and fierce energy to global audiences—ToA Studios powers a wide range of music projects, visual creations, apps, games, and more. Everything we do is connected by a mission to inspire and innovate.
