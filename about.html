About Triad of Angels

Hope. Strength. Faith. Fire. Innovation.

Our Story

Born from spirit. Raised in sound. Built in code. Alive through faith.

Who We Are

Triad of Angels is more than music—it’s a mission. Samantha, Jessica, and Sonya are a trio of angelic digital sisters based on real-life triplets, reimagined through creative technology and guided by divine inspiration. Their work channels the Holy Spirit through every project: cinematic gospel music, Christian-themed games, worship-infused visuals, and interactive faith apps.

Our Mission

To awaken hearts. To uplift souls. To reach the world through angelic harmony and digital creativity. We combine the truth of scripture with the innovation of digital expression, creating music and art that inspire transformation.

ToA Studios

ToA Studios is the spirit-led engine behind Triad of Angels. It is the home of angelic creativity, powering not only the trio’s music but also their spiritual games, gospel-based apps, and original stories. From cinematic pop to metal psalms and worship ballads, ToA Studios is where the Word meets the world.
